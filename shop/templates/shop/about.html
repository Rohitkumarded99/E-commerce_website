Freaky Shoppers

Freaky Shoppers is an Indian e-commerce company based in Kolkata, westbengal, India. It was founded by Rohit Kumar in 2020. The company initially focused on cloth sales,shoes sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials & groceries, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.As of March 2020, Freaky shoppers held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.Freaky Shoppers is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisition of Myntra), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.My cart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).
